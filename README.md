# 📊 Customer Behaviour Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior in an e-commerce dataset to generate actionable business insights. The goal is to understand revenue drivers, customer segments, and the impact of discounts and shipping strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify high-value customers
- Analyze the impact of discounts on purchasing behavior
- Understand revenue contribution across customer segments
- Evaluate category-wise and shipping-wise performance
- Support data-driven business decision-making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Python (Pandas)** → Data Cleaning & Preprocessing  
- **SQL** → Data Analysis & Insight Generation  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning (Python)
- Removed duplicate records  
- Handled missing values using logical imputation  
- Standardized categorical values  
- Created derived features (age groups, categories)  
- Ensured data consistency for analysis  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Concentration (Pareto Analysis)
- **~28.6% of customers contribute ~80% of total revenue**
- Indicates strong dependency on high-value customers  

### 2. Discount vs Purchase Behavior
- Discounted customers show **higher average purchase value**
- Discounts increase basket size but should be used strategically  

### 3. Age Group Contribution
- **51+ age group contributes ~40% of total revenue**
- Represents a high-value customer segment  

### 4. Shipping Type Impact
- **Express shipping users have the highest average purchase value**
- Faster delivery correlates with higher spending  

### 5. Category Performance
- **Electronics contributes ~49% of total revenue**
- High-value products dominate revenue generation  

### 6. Customer Purchase Frequency
- All customers appear as **one-time buyers**
- Dataset limitation: No repeat purchase data available  

---

## ⚠️ Limitations
- Dataset does not contain repeat transactions per customer  
- Customer retention and lifetime value analysis could not be performed  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on retaining high-value customers  
- Use targeted discount strategies instead of blanket discounts  
- Invest more in high-performing categories (Electronics)  
- Encourage premium shipping options to increase order value  
- Target high-spending age segments with personalized campaigns
